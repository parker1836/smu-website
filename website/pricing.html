Questions, answered

FAQ

If your question isn't here, write us. We answer every inquiry personally within 48 hours.

Do I need trading experience to apply?

No. About 80% of our students start with under six months of live trading. What we ask is that you're willing to go through the modules, study and put in the actual work.

Is this a get-rich-quick program?

No. We don't sell signals, we don't pitch a single setup that "always works," and we don't promise outcomes. We teach a process. What you do with it is on you.

How much time per week?

What you put into the course is what you'll get out of it. You can lead a horse to water, but you can't force it to drink. We recommend a minimum of at least one hour per day.

Do I need capital to start?

No, you don't need any capital to go through the course. We don't recommend you start trading until you're fully done with the modules. At that point, you'll need funds to start trading real capital — or you can start paper trading first.

What's your refund policy?

We do not give refunds. With so much information inside the course, someone could easily download and copy all of it, store it elsewhere, and ask for their money back. We can't give the blueprint away for free.
