Is Stock Market University right for you?

We're not for everyone — and we're proud of that. Read both sides carefully before you apply.

This is for you if...

You're ready to put in the work and become a real trader.

  • You're serious about building a long-term skill, not chasing a quick win.
  • You're coachable — willing to follow a proven system and trust the process.
  • You're disciplined enough to journal trades, learn from your losses, and own mistakes.
  • You can commit a few hours a week to learning, reviewing, and showing up.
  • You're tired of free YouTube videos and conflicting advice — you want one clear path.
  • You want financial freedom and you're willing to earn it the right way.
  • You want a mentor who's actually in the market — not just showing you their rented condo and rented Lambo in Miami.
This is not for you if...

You're looking for a shortcut or a guaranteed payout.

  • You want a "get-rich-quick" scheme or trading signals to copy blindly.
  • You expect guaranteed returns — the market doesn't work that way, and neither do we.
  • You're not willing to study, journal, and put real effort into your craft.
  • You can't handle losing trades — losses are part of the game, every trader takes them.
  • You won't follow a structured plan or trust the process when it gets uncomfortable.
  • You're using money you can't afford to risk — never trade your rent.
  • You'd rather blame the market than take ownership of your results.
